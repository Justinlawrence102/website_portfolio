2018 Cornell Theme Park Design Competitions

Tales of Tiz

In spring 2018, I worked with a team of 4 to compete in this design competition. The challenge was to create an attraction concept. We started by creating an original story and character. Our story was based on the fictional character Tiz, who is an legendary creature from Alaska. Then, we created story boards and images to show various scenes from the ride. The riders would fo through scenes of an alaskan wilderness on boats and use flashlights on the ride vehicle to interact with various creatures they pass. To demonstright how the scene would transform when Tiz appeared partway through the ride, I created a small model of one of the scenes and using projection mapping techniques to animate the scene.

Other aspects of the challange included making a budget for how much we think the attraction would cost to make, as well as a floorplan, which needed to include utilities and operational necessities.

Our team came in 3rd place out of over 50 teams from around the country and we had the opertuntiy to receieve feedback of out designs from industry professionals.
